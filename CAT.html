Happy ending stories

Rocco

Saved from the abuse of wicked children who wanted to throw him into the sea, thanks to the intervention of a volunteer. For over 10 years, Rocco has been enjoying the cosiness of home with the same family that took Miss in.

Fiocco

Fiocco wandered lonely through the streets. Poor Fiocco, he had almost certainly been abandoned! But thanks to the online ad published by the volunteers, it found a wonderful adoption in a villa with many other feline friends.

Miss

Found a few months old on a rainy October afternoon while finding precarious shelter under a car. Immediately rescued by volunteers from a local association, then groomed, wormed and given her first vaccine Miss was adopted by Rocco's family six years ago.

Valery

Valery was disliked by the neighbourhood. Some people hated her so much that they drove her away by throwing stones at her. It was imperative that she be saved! Stalled by those who had taken her in to save her from the street, in time she won the hearts of children.


What you can do


Against violence and abandonment, we can offer a forever family to our four-legged friends. Abandonment, kitten trafficking, mistreatment, every day thousands of cats are victims of unacceptable violence.

Adopt one of the cats we rescued and give it the most beautiful gift of all: to be part of a forever family!
